About Mason

A marketer first,
an agent always

I'm proud of the recognition — Platinum Top 50, Real Producers 500, and over $140M in career sales — but I built my business to serve clients with peerless marketing, clear guidance through the details that matter, and the hustle to find the right buyer for every property.

For sellers, I go well beyond the MLS. My team includes a full-time videographer producing lifestyle-first tours of every listing, no matter the price point. Video, strategic professional photography, and genuine effort to put your home in front of real buyers work together to sell for the most money possible.

For buyers, my goal is to make you confident through the entire process — the right location, the right home at the right price, and a contract handled backwards and forwards so your deposit stays protected and you stay excited, not stressed.
